About VidyaVault

Empowering students to showcase their academic excellence and connect with peers through innovative project sharing.

Our Mission

VidyaVault is designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application. We provide a comprehensive platform where college students can:

  • Upload and showcase their academic projects
  • Get evaluated by faculty and peers
  • Build a comprehensive academic portfolio
  • Connect with like-minded students
  • Gain recognition for their hard work

For Students

Create your academic portfolio, showcase your projects, and get recognized for your innovations. Connect with peers and learn from their work.

For Faculty

Review and evaluate student projects efficiently. Provide meaningful feedback and track student progress across different domains.

Key Features

Project Upload

Easy-to-use interface for uploading project details, GitHub repositories, and documentation.

Scoring System

Comprehensive evaluation based on innovation, completeness, design, and GitHub usage.

Analytics

Detailed charts and statistics showing department performance and technology trends.

Community

Comment system for Q&A, discussions, and peer-to-peer learning.

Scoring Criteria

Projects are evaluated by faculty members based on four key criteria:

Innovation (25%)

Creativity, uniqueness, and novel approach to problem-solving.

Completeness (25%)

Project functionality, documentation quality, and feature implementation.

Design (25%)

User interface, user experience, and overall aesthetic appeal.

GitHub Usage (25%)

Code quality, commit history, documentation, and repository organization.
